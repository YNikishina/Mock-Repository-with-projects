**Manhattan Airbnb Listings Analysis**

Project Overview

This project analyzes Airbnb listings in Manhattan with the goal of identifying the most attractive neighborhoods and listing types for short-term rental investment.

The analysis focuses on revenue potential, review activity, neighborhood performance, and listing characteristics to determine which properties generate the strongest business results.

This project demonstrates practical skills in spreadsheet analysis, business thinking, data cleaning, pivot tables, and decision-making based on real-world marketplace data.

---

Business Goal

Help a potential investor understand:

	*	Which Manhattan neighborhoods perform best for Airbnb investments
	*	Which listing types generate the highest revenue
	*	What characteristics define top-performing listings
	*	Where to focus investment efforts for maximum return

---

Dataset

The dataset contains Airbnb listings information for Manhattan, including:

Key Findings

Strongest Investment Areas

Midtown and several high-demand Manhattan neighborhoods consistently showed the strongest revenue potential due to high review volume and strong occupancy indicators.

Listing Type Performance

	*	Studio (0-bedroom) listings performed best in Midtown
	*	1-bedroom listings showed stronger performance in most other top neighborhoods

Revenue Drivers

High-performing listings were driven more by occupancy and review volume than by nightly price alone.

This suggests that sustainable demand matters more than premium pricing.

---

Business Recommendations

Recommended Investment Focus

	*	Prioritize Midtown for studio investments
	*	Focus on 1-bedroom properties in other top-performing neighborhoods
	*	Evaluate listings based on long-term demand indicators rather than price only

Risk Reduction

Avoid relying solely on high-price listings with low review activity, as they may indicate weak occupancy and unstable returns.
